...a poet, writer, & a cartoonist



A poem from Silverstein's book: Everything On It



His unique imagination and bold brand of humor is beloved by countless adults and children throughout the world. Silverstein is best known as the author of iconic books of prose and poetry for young readers. His works include A Light in the Attic, Where the Sidewalk Ends, The Giving Tree, and Runny Babbit He illustrated his own books with a mix of silly and serious topics. He was also an accomplished musician and composer writing songs used by Johnny Cash. He also wrote and published playwrigths that were popular. Sadly, Silverstein passed away in 1999 but his amazing work lives on and is still inspiring many.
