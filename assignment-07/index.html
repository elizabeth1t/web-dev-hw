Western Montana Wildflowers

Western Montana has a wide variety of wildflower types. These wildflowers grow at differnt altitudes, soil types, and in differnt environments. These perrenial wildflowers are dormant in the winter but start to flower again in spring.

Some common types of wildflowers:

  • Arnica
  • Bear Grass
  • Bluebells
  • Bitterroot
  • Deptford Pink
